AudioCoin is a listen-to-earn platform. Users must be 18+. Earned coins can be withdrawn above the minimum threshold of 500 AC. Privacy Policy

We collect name, email, phone number, country/state and listening activity. We comply with the Nigeria Data Protection Regulation (NDPC). We never sell your data. Data is stored securely in Firebase with encryption at rest.

Payout Policy

Minimum withdrawal: 2,000 AC (approximately 7 days of consistent listening). All withdrawals are processed via FaucetPay to the user's registered FaucetPay account. Users can choose Bitcoin (BTC), Litecoin (LTC), Dogecoin (DOGE), Dash, TRON (TRX), or Ethereum (ETH). Withdrawals are processed within 24 hours.
